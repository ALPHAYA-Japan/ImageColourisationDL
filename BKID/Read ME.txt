How to use:

network.py consists of the model structure.
latest.h5 is the weights and biases for our last known results.
Use train.py to train the network.
Use evaluate.py to color images and videos.
Argument parser is available.. So just use -h and wait to get the available options.

Dependencies required:

Python 3.x
Ananconda (VirtualEnv preferred)
TensorFlow
NumPy
Keras
OpenCV

The rest are already installed with Anaconda.
